Privacy policy

Effective July 17, 2026 · TidyMap AI is built by SCM Solutions LLC

TidyMap helps you organize a space in your home. To do that, it needs a few things from you. This page explains, in plain language, what we collect, what we do with it, and what we never do.

What we collect

  • Photos and videos you upload. These are sent to our server for AI analysis so we can build your plan. If you are not signed in, they stay in your browser and are not stored on our servers after the analysis. If you sign in and save a space, your photos and plan are stored in your private account so you can come back to them.
  • Your email address. Only if you sign in (we email you a one-time code, there are no passwords) or request an invitation to the Founding Circle.
  • Your answers in the planner. The space type, goals, measurements, and household details (like whether kids use the space) that you enter. These shape your plan. They are stored only if you save a space.
  • Feedback you submit. Exactly what you type in the feedback form.
  • Basic anti-abuse data. To prevent abuse of the free AI analysis, our server keeps a short-lived, salted hash of your IP address. The salt changes daily, so these hashes cannot be linked back to you or connected across days. We do not store raw IP addresses.

How the AI works with your photos

When you request an analysis, your photos are sent through our server to Anthropic (the company behind the Claude AI model) to produce your organization plan. If you ask for a photo preview of your organized space, the photo is also sent to Google (the Gemini image model). Both act as processors for us: they process your photos to provide the feature you asked for. Our API keys stay on the server, never in your browser.

Where your data lives

  • Supabase hosts our database, sign-in, and file storage. Saved spaces and photos are protected so only your account can read them.
  • GitHub Pages hosts this website.
  • Google Fonts serves the site's fonts, which means your browser requests a file from Google when the page loads.
  • Drafts of unsaved plans live in your own browser's local storage, on your device, not on our servers.

What we never do

  • We never sell your data or your photos.
  • We never use your photos for advertising or to train our own models.
  • We never share your data with anyone except the service providers named above, and only to provide the features you use.
  • We do not run ads or third-party trackers on this site.

Product links

Plans can include links to storage products at retailers like Amazon, Target, and Walmart. If we ever join a retailer's affiliate program, we may earn a commission on qualifying purchases and a disclosure will appear next to those links. Prices stay the same for you either way. Retailers have their own privacy policies once you are on their site.

Kids

TidyMap is for adults. We ask whether children use your space only so plans can keep hazards out of reach and snacks within reach. The site is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their information.

Your choices

  • You can use TidyMap without an account. Guest plans stay in your browser, and you can clear them anytime by clearing your browser data or tapping Start over.
  • You can delete saved spaces from your dashboard when signed in.
  • You can ask us to delete your account and everything tied to it, or to show you what we have stored about you.

Contact

Questions or deletion requests: reach SCM Solutions LLC through scmsolutions.org. We answer every request.

Changes

If this policy changes, we will update this page and the date at the top. Meaningful changes will be called out on the site, not buried.
